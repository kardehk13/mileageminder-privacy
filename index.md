     ## Data Storage

     All your data is stored locally on your device and, if enabled, in your private iCloud account via CloudKit. Your data is
     never stored on UCS Managed Services servers.

     **iCloud Sync:** If you have iCloud enabled, your drive data syncs across your Apple devices through your private iCloud
     account. Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud storage. UCS Managed Services cannot access your iCloud data.

     ## Data Sharing

     We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Drive data classified as "Personal" is never
     included in any reports you generate.

     ## Data Retention

     Your data remains on your device and iCloud until you delete it. You can delete all data at any time via Settings → Data →
     Delete All Data within the app.

     ## Third-Party Services

     MileageMinder uses the following Apple frameworks:
     - **CoreLocation** — GPS tracking
     - **CloudKit** — private iCloud sync
     - **MapKit** — address search and map display

     No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.
